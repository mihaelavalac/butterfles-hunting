Game instructions:

  • Welcome to Butterflies Hunting!
  • To begin the game click the START button in the Game tab.
  • After the START button was clicked you will have 30 seconds to catch as many butterflies as you can.
  • Click the butterfly image to catch it.
  • Each butterfly is worth 1 point.
  • When the time will pass, your score will be displayed on the screen.
